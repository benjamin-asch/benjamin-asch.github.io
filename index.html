About

I am a Master's student in Quantum Engineering at ETH Zürich. Broadly, I am interested in quantum information, optimization, and the geometry of variational quantum algorithms, especially in regimes where tools from analysis, probability, and theoretical computer science all interact.

Before ETH, I studied Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences (with a minor in mathematics) at UC Berkeley. Recently I have been thinking about provable guarantees for VQE-style methods, how low-rank structure in the Hessian affects the landscape, and when this structure can be turned into concrete sample and runtime improvements.

Quantum rankings project

I am working on a small side project that uses publication and conference data to explore research strength in quantum information and computation across institutions.

The goal is not to produce a single “leaderboard”, but to give people a way to slice the data themselves: by subarea, venue, or time window.
